Aspects

Aspects determine how your decks will generally function. Here's an overview of the six aspects currently present in the game.

Aspect Penalties

No deck is required to follow its aspects. Your decks have the aspects of your leader and your base. However, should you decide to stray from your chosen path, you will be penalized. For every aspect you are out of aspect, you will be required to pay 2. It's also worth noting that some cards are double aspected. Double aspected cards require both your base and leader to have the same aspect, lest you wish to suffer any penalties.

Neutral

  • - Usable in any deck
  • - Early-Midgame cards
  • - Tend to be weak for their cost
  • - Very few rares

Heroism/Villainy represents the core of Star Wars Unlimited. All leaders are either Heroic or Villainous.

Heroism

  • + Stronger early game
  • + Restore
  • + Easier access to Force units
  • + Coordinate
  • +/- Clone Troopers are tougher than Battle Droids, but harder to produce.

Villainy

  • + Powerful late game
  • + Easier to Ramp
  • + Bounty interaction
  • +/- Exploit cards
  • +/- Battle Droids are easily mass produced, but fragile.

Heroism/Vigilance

  • + Restore
  • + Force synergy
  • + Defensive Units

Vigilance

  • More defensive cards
  • Units are tougher
  • Upgrade heavy
  • Instant death spells
  • Late Game

Vigilance/Villainy

  • + Powerful Endgame
  • + Instant death

  • - Early Game
